What is SMIRC?

SMIRC (Short Message Internet Relay Chat) is a fusion of Short Message Service (also known as "SMS" or "texting" to most any user of a cellphone) and Internet Relay Chat. SMIRC's goal is to ease the use of texting between groups of people: instead of sending a text to a person, on SMIRC a text is sent to a room of people.
